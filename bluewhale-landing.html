How It Works

Three layers of intelligence.
One platform.

01
📡

Detect

Satellite imagery (Sentinel-2, free & open) is analyzed by Gemini Vision AI every 5 days per zone. Citizen photo reports fill the gaps in real time. Every data point is validated and geotagged automatically.

Gemini Vision + Satellite
02
🧠

Analyze

AI calculates pollution type, severity score, species risk, and fishing pressure per zone. Ecosystem health score is computed in real time and updated continuously — not in monthly reports.

AI Health Score Engine
03
🚨

Alert

Direct automated alerts go to coast guard, pollution boards, and NGOs — not reports, fire alarms. Fishermen get SMS zone advisories with no smartphone needed. Green / Yellow / Red zones daily.

SMS + Authority Alerts
Features

Built to act,
not just report.

Every feature is designed around one principle: close the gap between detection and action.

🌊

Pollution Scanner

Upload any ocean or coastal photo. Gemini Vision identifies pollution type, estimates affected area, calculates severity score, and recommends immediate action — in under 5 seconds.

🗺️

Live Hotspot Map

3D interactive map showing real-time pollution clusters, biodiversity risk zones, and fishing pressure data. Authorities see exactly where to deploy resources — no guesswork.

🐋

Species Watch

Continuous AI monitoring of underwater camera feeds. When species count drops 20% from baseline, zone is automatically flagged as at-risk before population collapse.

📱

Fisherman SMS Advisory

No smartphone needed. Fisherman texts their zone. BlueWhale replies with safe/unsafe status and suggests better zones. Works on any basic phone, anywhere in India.

📶

Offline First

Gemma 3n runs entirely on-device when internet is unavailable. Critical for remote marine protected areas, offshore monitoring stations, and coastal fieldwork.

⚡

Automated Alert Chain

When severity crosses threshold, BlueWhale doesn't write a report — it sends a direct alert to the right authority. Like a fire alarm, not a fire investigation.
